About CBIT NSS


Chaitanya Seva

NSS: NSS CBIT was established in 2007, bringing together students from various departments. Currently, the NSS body includes 150 UG and 70 PG students, led by a 24-member student executive team. They actively coordinate and participate in numerous social service activities both within the institute and the larger community.

Street Cause: Founded in 2009, Street Cause is an NGO comprising students dedicated to social betterment. It is one of CBITâ€™s longest-running clubs, offering a platform for young individuals to contribute towards meaningful social change. Street Cause has over 50 divisions in different colleges, with CBIT being among the most active.

Engineers Without Borders (EWB): EWB-CBIT was founded in 2015 by Dr. Shaukat Mirza with a vision to nurture students and professionals for real-life sustainable social projects, shaping them into global leaders.

NCC: The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at CBIT provides military training, instilling discipline, leadership, and patriotism. NCC cadets actively participate in national-level camps and social initiatives, enhancing both personal and professional growth.

Parivrita: This initiative focuses on holistic development through awareness campaigns, educational drives, and environmental initiatives. Parivrita encourages students to engage in meaningful social service while building leadership skills.
